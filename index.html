What is the Champions League?

The Champions League is the greatest football tournament in the world, featuring the best teams in Europe. It is organized by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA)

It is a common practice that if a player from any country is exceptionally good they will be bought by an european club most of the times.

If the player is extremely good, then he will 100% play in one of the teams participating in this competition

How does it work?

The Champions League takes place every year and participating teams are qualified directly from their respective country leagues. Also, there are 4 more available slots for teams to qualify via a pre-tournament qualifyer played months before the start of the competition.

Each country has a certain ammount of slots depending on league's overall FIFA position aka best leagues get more qualifying slots

First there is a group stage consisting of 8 groups with 4 teams on each group

Then there is the knockout stage consisting in two elimination games for each match, one home and one away, aggregate score decides who passes to next stage
